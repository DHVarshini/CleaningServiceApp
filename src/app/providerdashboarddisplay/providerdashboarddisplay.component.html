Attention to detail
A cleaning service provider needs to have an eye for detail, ensuring that each inch of the property is cleaned thoroughly.
Consistency
A high-quality cleaning service provider should aim to provide consistent cleaning services each time, ensuring that the property is always cleaned to the same high standard.
Use of the right equipment and products
A good cleaning service provider should use the correct equipment for each task, and safe cleaning products to ensure that the property is cleaned properly and safely.

Our security Policies

  1. Background checks:Conduct a thorough background check on all employees before hiring them, including criminal records and employment history.
  2. Confidentiality agreements:All employees must sign a confidentiality agreement to protect clients' personal and financial information.
  3. Employee training:Train employees in security and safety practices to prevent theft, damage or injury to both employees and clients.
  4. Access control:Only authorized employees must have access to clients' keys, and keys must be kept safe and secure.
  5. Communication with clients: Maintain privacy and security of clients at all times, communicate any concerns or observations immediately with the client or management.
  6. Proper equipment and supplies:Use only approved cleaning equipment and supplies, and maintain it properly.
  7. Insurance:Ensure the cleaning service provider has proper insurance coverage to protect their employees, clients, and property in case of accidents or incidents.
  8. Documentation and record keeping:Maintain a record of client information, employee records, and security incidents, and ensure they're kept confidential.
  9. Policies and procedures: Clearly outline security policies and procedures and communicate them with all employees.
  10. Emergency response plan:Develop an emergency response plan in case of security breaches or other emergencies that might arise.
