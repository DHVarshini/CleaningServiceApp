Our security Policies

  1. Background checks: All employees must undergo a thorough background check before being hired, including criminal records and employment history.
  2. Confidentiality agreements: All employees must sign a confidentiality agreement to protect clients' personal and financial information.
  3. Employee training: All employees must be trained in security and safety practices to prevent theft, damage, or injury to both employees and clients.
  4. Keys and access: Clients' keys must be kept safe and secure, and only authorized employees should have access to them.
  5. Communication with clients: Client privacy and security must be maintained at all times, and employees should communicate any concerns or observations immediately to the client or management.
  6. Proper equipment and supplies: Only approved cleaning supplies and equipment should be used, and all equipment must be properly maintained and secured when not in use.
  7. Insurance: The cleaning service must have proper insurance coverage to protect their employees, clients, and property in case of accidents or incidents.
  8. Documentation and record keeping: All client information, employee records, and security incidents should be documented and kept confidential.
  9. Policies and procedures: Security policies and procedures should be clearly outlined and communicated to all employees, and regular training and updates should be provided.
  10. Emergency response plan: The cleaning service should have an emergency response plan in place in case of security breaches or other emergencies.
